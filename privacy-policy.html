Privacy Policy for X Video Tracker

Last Updated: October, 2023

Summary: X Video Tracker is a privacy-focused Chrome extension that stores all your video watching data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on our servers.

Introduction

X Video Tracker is a Chrome extension designed to help users track their progress on videos watched on Twitter/X platform and resume playback from where they left off. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

Information We Collect

X Video Tracker collects the following information when you watch videos on Twitter/X:

  • Video playback progress (timestamp where you stopped watching)
  • Video metadata (duration, URL, title)
  • Thumbnail images from the videos you watch
  • Your folder organization for saved videos

How We Use Your Information

The information collected by X Video Tracker is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To remember where you left off in a video so you can resume playback
  • To organize your watched videos in folders
  • To display thumbnails and metadata in the extension popup

Where Your Data Is Stored

All data collected by X Video Tracker is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API. Your data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

The extension operates entirely within your browser and does not have any backend server component. Your video watching habits and preferences remain private to you.

Data Retention

X Video Tracker automatically removes video entries that are older than one month to keep your storage clean. You can also manually delete any saved videos at any time through the extension popup.

Third-Party Access

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to any third parties. The extension does not include any analytics or tracking tools.

Extension Permissions

X Video Tracker requests the following permissions, and here's why:

  • storage: To save your video progress information locally on your device
  • tabs: To detect when Twitter/X tabs are open and track video progress
  • Host permissions for twitter.com and x.com: To interact with videos on these domains

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your data: You can clear all saved video data by removing the extension
  • Access your data: All your data is accessible through the extension popup
  • Control your data: You can manually delete individual videos or folders at any time

Children's Privacy

X Video Tracker is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the Chrome Web Store listing and within the extension. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
